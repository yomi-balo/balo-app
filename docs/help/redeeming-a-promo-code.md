# Redeeming a promo code

Have a Balo promo code? Redeeming it adds prepaid credit to your company — no card and no
payment required.

## How to redeem

From your dashboard, open the "Have a promo code?" card to reach the redeem screen — or go
straight to **/redeem**.

Type your code (codes aren't case-sensitive) and choose **Redeem**. You only need to be an
owner or admin of your company to redeem — if you aren't, ask a teammate who is.

## What "A$X added" means

When a code is redeemed, we add that amount of **prepaid Balo credit** to your company's
balance. It's yours to put towards a project, a Quick Start, or a live consultation
whenever you're ready. There's nothing to pay and no card on file — the credit is simply
there to use.

Each code can be redeemed **once per company**. If you enter a code your company already
redeemed, we'll let you know and nothing is added twice.

## When your promo balance runs out

Your credit works as a warm stopping point: once the promo balance is used up, work pauses
until you choose to keep going.

To continue, add a card from the redeem screen. **Nothing is charged when you add it** — we
only use the card once your promo balance runs out, and only when you decide to continue.
Until then, there's no charge and no commitment.
